Submit a bulk claim

Use this service to make monthly submissions for payments covering legal help, crime lower, and mediation.

Your submission will be checked and calculated.

Submissions, including nil submissions, must be received by the 20th of the month to trigger your monthly payment.

Before you start

Files can be in XML, CSV or TXT format. You can create them using your own case management system or you can use the bulkload spreadsheet. The spreadsheet can be found at Submit a Bulk Claim (SaBC) guidance page (opens in new tab) along with guidance for help on how to prepare it.
