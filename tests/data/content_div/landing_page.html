Submit a bulk claim

Use this service to make monthly submissions for payments covering legal help, crime lower, and mediation.

Your submission will be checked and calculated.

Submissions, including nil submissions, must be received by the 20th of the month to trigger your monthly payment.

Before you start

You will need a separate bulk claim file for each office and area of law you are claiming for. The maximum size is 10MB per file.

Files can be an XML, CSV, or TXT that have been created by your case management system, or use the bulkload spreadsheet (CSV, 9.99MB). You can also use the bulkload spreadsheet guide (PDF, 814KB) for help on how to prepare it.
